WHO AM I?

My name is Matt Kemball, there isn’t much to say about me besides that I am from Canada. Gatineau, Quebec Canada to be exact. I am a classically trained illustrator who studied Fine Arts at the Ottawa School of Art. I have always had an interest in comics, and finally got the opportunity to design, draw, and publish my own. This process led me to pursue graphic design, feeling that it would give me a better understanding of layout and typography.

WHAT I DO.

What I do is approach problems with a unique illustrative style. Working things out until they are perfect. My focus has mainly been in advertising for events, comic books, branding, and animation. Regardless of the discipline my style is distinctive through out. Below you will find examples of my work both for clients and for myself.
