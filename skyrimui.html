SKYRIM MENU UI

THE JOB

This was the redesign of Skyrim's main menu interface. The goal was to give a good first impression of the game, while also improving on the UI, creating smooth animations with a gameplay feel.

CHALLENGE

The challenge was to make the UI seem believable. This mean't entering realms I have never touched on before, such as using sound effects and video transitions. In the end everything came together.
