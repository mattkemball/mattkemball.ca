THE JOB

Witcher the Animated Series was done as an animation exercise. Mixing vector illustraion and video effects to create an illusion of depth. The scene is meant to tell a short story using these elements. Below is the final video along with the vector drawings, and my storyboards. The Witcher is a polish book and game series, I dont not own these this was merely an exercise.

CHALLENGE

The concept began as two heroes going to hunt a monster, but that became to ambitious to complete in two weeks. It was scaled down to the heroes entering a cave and facing the monster. Eventually I turned to the popular polish series The Witcher for inspiration. Thats when the project found its theme. The final challenge was to tell a story and show my concept in 10 second or less.
