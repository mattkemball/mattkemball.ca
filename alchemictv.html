ALCHEMIC TV

THE JOB

It began with picking a brand. Since I had already created an established indy comic book brand I decided to use it to create a fictional original animated content application, thus Alchemic TV was created. A broadcast package featuring brand elements was then to be created. Things such as lower thirds, promotionals, and episode openings were featured in the video below.

CHALLENGE

The greatest challenge was to create all the content within the span of a week. All the art work was carefully created over the course of two days. This included storyboards, the design of the various brand elements, and any other drawings that needed to be done. The fact that the artwork and design elements came out great in such a short span of time proved my ability to handle the pressure and think clearly.

  • NOTE: The cartoon featured in the video is an anime titled Cowboy Bebop created by Sunrise. it was meant to be representational of the content of this application.
