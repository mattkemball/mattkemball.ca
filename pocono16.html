POCONO COMIC EXPO 2016

THE JOB

The theme for this years expo was story telling. The idea was to invoke a sense of wonder and excitement through the advertising. This led to the eventual goal of telling a story through the imagery. Four slogans were conceived using four buzzwords: ADVENTURE, LEGEND, INCREDIBLE, and ODDS. The campaign started with the creation of four posters including 11x17 posters and 8x11 posters. These designs would serve as the base for the campaign going forward.

CHALLENGE

The challenge was to figure out how to tell a story using only a single image. Taking the slogans I set about brainstorming what could be done. The solution needed to evoke a feeling of excitment from the viewer and make them believe there was more to the images than what they were seeing. Several things came to mind, from science fiction, to fantasy, and even childhood fairy tales. Inspiration was also taken from other sources such as video games and cartoons. All this would lead to the creation of four posters, which would serve as the base of the advertising. Another challenge was to place all the information for the program onto an 11x17 sheet folded in half where one page was devoted to a map. A type hierarchy was determined and all the information was able to fit while remianing organized and readable.
